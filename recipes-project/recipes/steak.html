Pan-seared steak


A bit, juicy steak, perfect for any occasion!
Ingredients needed:
  • Beef cut of choice
  • Salt + pepper
  • 2tbsp Butter
  • Optional: Rosemary/miscellaneous spices and herbs
Steps:
  1. Season beef generously with salt + pepper, then wait for a while (for better flavor)
  2. Melt butter in a hot pan (along with other herbs, if desired)
  3. Place beef in pan, flipping occasionally for even cook
  4. Once desired internal temperature has been reached, take steak off pan. Let rest for up to 5 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
